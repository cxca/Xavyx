Rules of the game

--Rules can change at any time--

1-You only can upload up to five(5) pictures a day.

2-For each like(+1) the time of the picture will increase for five(5) more hours.

3-Top 10 pictures of the day will increase it's time for 24 hours more.

4-Top 50 pictures of the week will increase it's time for three(3) more days.
